This is my first game


Real Scratch Game with Jimmy Pham (currently in beta) official release date: 11-20-17

The purpose is to provide a fast-paced competitive two button two player game that will please the general audience

We used x and y velocities to program the jumping characters and we also created hitboxes so that the game would register a vctory or defeat. We had trouble with making the characters freeze upon touching. We fixed that by adding an if-then block to the x and y velocities and adding a stop variable. The current bugs of the game are features.

Three friends were enjoying their time together, playing video games. Then all of a sudden, an intruder breaks into their home. Playing as the main character, Tyler, you must work with your friends to deal with that threat.
